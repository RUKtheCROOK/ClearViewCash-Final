§ 01

The Spaces Model

Every user owns their own accounts. They join (or create) Spaces with other people, then independently decide which accounts and transactions to share inside that space. A space switcher in the header lets users move between contexts seamlessly.

User A
  • Chase Checking · shared
  • Citi Card · shared
  • Personal Savings · private
  • Side-Project LLC · private
User B
  • Wells Checking · shared
  • Amex Card · shared
  • Roth IRA · private
  • Gift Fund · private
Shared Space "Household" — only the ⇄ items appear here. Toggle: My View ⇄ Shared View
§ 02

Payment Links

A "Pays For" relationship between a funding account and one or more credit cards. The funding account's effective available = current balance minus the running total of all linked card balances. Updates in real time as transactions post — no waiting for statement close.

Account 1 · Checking
  • Current Balance · $3,400
  • Pays → Card A · $1,200
  • Pays → Card B · $400
  • Effective Available · $1,800
Account 2 · Checking
  • Current Balance · $2,100
  • Pays → Card C · $650
  • No other links
  • Effective Available · $1,450
Per-Link Config Each link: which account, which card(s), split %, space scope. Default · Single Space  |  Optional · Cross-Space
RULE 01

Optional, Not Required

Users without a clear funding-to-card relationship use the app the standard way. Payment Links are an opt-in power feature.

RULE 02

Many to Many

One account can pay multiple cards. One card can be paid by multiple accounts (with split percentages).

RULE 03

Cross-Space Aware

Default scope is single-space. Users can explicitly enable cross-space linking when a personal account also funds household cards.

RULE 04

Real-Time Math

Tracks current balance, not statement. The moment a charge posts, effective available drops. No surprises at statement close.

§ 04

Page Anatomy

What lives on each primary screen. Every page respects the active space's "My View ⇄ Shared View" toggle.

01

Dashboard

The at-a-glance financial pulse for whichever space is active. First thing the user sees on open.

Key Modules
  • Net worth snapshot card
  • Cash on hand · total debt
  • Effective available cash (live)
  • Funding coverage status
  • Upcoming bills (7d / 30d)
  • Recent transaction feed
  • Budget progress at a glance
  • Goal progress widgets
  • View toggle: Mine / Shared
02

Transactions

Unified ledger across all linked accounts. The most-used screen — must be fast and filterable.

Key Capabilities
  • Pending vs. completed filter
  • Filter by account / category / person
  • Per-transaction share toggle
  • Edit category · add notes
  • Mark as recurring
  • Split transactions
  • Search
03

Bills

Every obligation in one place. Calendar and list views. Auto-detects recurring patterns from transactions.

Key Capabilities
  • Calendar + list views
  • Recurring vs one-time
  • Paid / unpaid / overdue states
  • Autopay flag per bill
  • Pattern-detection suggestions
  • Linked to source transactions
04

Income

Mirror of Bills. Tracks expected income, recurring paychecks, and one-time deposits.

Key Capabilities
  • Recurring income (paychecks)
  • One-time income
  • Expected vs. received
  • Next payday countdown
  • Income source breakdown
05

Cash Flow

Differentiator feature. Projects your balance forward in time using known bills, income, and linked card payments.

Key Capabilities
  • Projected balance timeline
  • Daily / weekly / monthly granularity
  • Per-account or aggregate
  • Models linked card payments as outflows
  • "What if" scenarios
  • Low-balance & coverage warnings
06

Budgets

Category-based monthly spending limits. Per-space, so household budgets are separate from personal.

Key Capabilities
  • Categories with monthly caps
  • Progress bars · % spent
  • Rollover settings
  • Per-space budgets
  • Suggested categories from history
07

Goals

Savings targets and debt payoff plans, with date projections.

Key Capabilities
  • Savings goals
  • Debt payoff goals
  • Payoff date projections
  • Per-space or per-user
  • Progress visualization
08

Reports

Deeper analysis and exports. Custom date ranges, multiple report types, downloadable.

Key Capabilities
  • Cash flow report
  • Spending by category
  • Net worth over time
  • Custom date ranges
  • PDF / CSV export
  • QuickBooks sync (v2)
09

Accounts

Master list of every linked institution. Inline indicators show payment links between accounts and cards. Sharing settings live here too.

Key Capabilities
  • Linked accounts (Plaid)
  • Balance · sync status
  • Effective available (with links)
  • Payment Links setup & edit
  • "Pays For" / "Paid By" indicators
  • Per-account share settings
  • Reconnect broken links
  • QuickBooks connection (v2)
10

Settings

Profile, spaces management, security, subscription. Everything that's not day-to-day money work.

Sub-Pages
  • Profile & account
  • Spaces & members
  • Notifications
  • Security · 2FA · biometrics
  • Subscription & billing
  • Connected services
